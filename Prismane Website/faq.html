What is the workflow

- We ask you a few questions about your company
- A visual mockup is created and the client can order changes
- Any information from the company (menus, products etc.) is sent to us
- The website is coded
- Client goes over new website and order necessary changes
- Domain is transferred or bought, files uploaded to webhost
- Payment is made (if necessary)
- Website is online!
